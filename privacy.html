Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PULSEWIRE does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app runs entirely on your device. Your game progress, best scores, and settings are saved locally on your device and are never sent to us or any third party.

The app makes no network connections. It contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind, and it does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other personal information.

Data we collect

None. Because no data is collected, there is nothing for us to access, modify, or delete. Removing the app clears its local data from your device.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
